Our Services

Reservations

Reservations are typically required due to limited seating and the personalized nature of the experience.

Chef's Choice

The menu is entirely at the discretion of the chef. Each course is carefully curated and designed to highlight the natural flavors of the ingredients and showcase the chef's culinary skills.

Unique Dining Experience:

Asahina Sushi crafts a distinct, lavish experience with exceptional cuisine, curated ambiance, and meticulous attention to detail, immersing guests in luxury.

Expertise in Omasake

Asahina Sushi is known for its expertise in Omasake-style sushi. This style involves skilled chefs selecting and preparing a customized tasting menu with the freshest seasonal ingredients. We create a culinary journey that showcases a variety of flavors, textures, and presentations, highlighting the essence of each ingredient in a remarkable way.

Sake Pairing

Asahina Sushi stands out with its emphasis on pairing sushi with perfectly matched sake. We offer a wide selection of premium sake, and our knowledgeable staff helps customers choose the best sake to enhance their sushi flavors.

Our Vision

Our company vision is to redefine the sushi experience with unmatched luxury, culinary expertise, and a commitment to customer satisfaction. Indulge in Omasake-style sushi and sake pairing, embracing authentic flavors and traditions of Japan.
